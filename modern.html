DIWALI TAKING THE FORM OF MODERNITY:

While nowadays every festival is taking the form of modernism, similarly Diwali has adopted the present form. Where earlier on the occasion of Deepawali, people used to decorate their houses with earthen lamps. Today, candles and Chinese lights have replaced those lamps. Not only this, but Deepawali has also become a festival of showings of “Shan-o-Shaukat” in today’s time. People spend a lot of time on show-off to each other, by the decorating of the house, etc. Apart from this, fireworks on the occasion of Diwali cause great harm to the environment, there is also a waste of money. Pollution increases greatly on Diwali, which has a very bad effect on health. At the same time, all of us should resolve to plant new trees instead of firecrackers and celebrate green eco-friendly Diwali.
