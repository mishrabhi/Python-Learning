WHEN IS DIWALI CELEBRATED?

This festival of Deepawali is one of the most prominent festivals of the Hindus is celebrated with great pomp on the new moon day of Kartik month according to the Hindu calendar. On the day of this Mahaparva, the dark night of Amavasya shines with countless lamps. The glare of Lights is seen everywhere. This festival comes between the months of October or November every year. This is the festival of happiness and prosperity, which lasts for 5 days, it starts on the day of Mahaparva Dhanteras and ends on Bhaidooj. Also, read Diwali Essay in 1000 words.
