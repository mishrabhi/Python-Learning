HOW IS DIWALI CELEBRATED?

The festival of Deepawali is one of the biggest and major festivals of Hindus, various events are organized on the occasion of this festival. On this day, people decorate their homes in a special way and this festival celebrated for 5 days starts on Dhanteras and ends on the festival of Bhai Dooj. In Dhanteras, the Mahaparava of Deepawali starts with Dhanteras. On this day of Dhanteras, people bring new utensils, gold-silver items or other items to their homes by buying them. It is believed that by buying items on the day of Dhanteras, the money is increased in the house. One day before Diwali is small Diwali, people get up early morning, take bath and worship the Sun. People also worship Mata Lakshmi and Ganesh on the day of Diwali and worshipping Goddess Saraswati, the goddess of learning, also has special significance on this day.
